About the Artist

Beatriz Jalvo is a passionate illustrator who brings imagination to life through detailed and emotional artwork. Inspired by fantasy, nature, and childhood wonder, her style blends traditional and digital techniques.

Her illustrations are meant to spark curiosity, tell stories, and evoke the magic of the everyday world seen through a childlike lens.

Since childhood, Beatriz has been captivated by drawing, colors, and the power of storytelling. She dreamed of creating worlds where others could momentarily lose themselves, not to escape reality but to find comfort, inspiration and wonder. Through her art, she offers a window into the imaginative spirit she's nurtured since day one.
